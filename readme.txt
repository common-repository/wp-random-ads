== Description ==

<p>WP Random Ads Plugin allows you to manage advertising on the post (single post) randomly.</p>

<p>By using the plugin WP Random Ads will help you position the ads on your posts (on a single post).</p>

<p>There are 27 layouts that will randomly change every visitor refreshes the page. And you can also easily layout settings like what you want.</p>

<p>You can display a variety of ad: Google Adsense, affiliate ads, banners, images, flash, text, etc ...</p>

<p>Contact me for say hello <a href="http://onnayokheng.com">Onnay Okheng</a> or buy me a cup of chocolate :)</p>

== Installation ==

Upload the WP Random Ads plugin to your blog, Activate it.

1, 2, 3: You're done!
